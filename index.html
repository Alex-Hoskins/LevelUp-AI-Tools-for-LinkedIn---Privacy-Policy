Aria

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Aria ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") is a browser extension designed to help users create, improve, and manage professional content and messages using AI-powered tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your information is handled.

2. Data Collection

We collect limited, anonymous usage data to improve the extension. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), message content, resume data, or sensitive platform data.

  • Anonymous Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics events (such as extension opened or feature used) to understand product usage and improve functionality.
  • OpenAI API Key: Your API key is stored only in your browser using Chrome local storage. It is never transmitted to Aria or any third-party server.
  • Local Storage Data: Your message history, resume text, templates, preferences, and profile-related data are stored exclusively on your device.
  • Platform Data (Read-Only, On-Demand):
    • Name and headline are accessed only when needed to auto-fill templates and are not stored.
    • Job descriptions are accessed only when you trigger relevant features and are stored temporarily in your browser.
    • Messages are accessed only when you request AI-generated replies and are stored temporarily.

3. How AI Features Work

  • All AI requests are sent directly from your browser to OpenAI using your API key.
  • Aria does not act as a proxy and does not see, store, or log your prompts or responses.

4. Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics.

  • Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • No message content, platform data, resume data, or API keys are included.
  • No advertising networks are used.
  • Your data is never sold.

5. Use of Permissions

  • scripting: Read page content for user-triggered features.
  • tabs: Detect relevant pages.
  • storage: Save data locally.
  • contextMenus: Provide right-click actions.
  • sidePanel: Display the interface.
  • activeTab: Interact with the current page when triggered.

6. Third-Party Services

OpenAI: AI features send requests directly from your browser. View Privacy Policy.

PostHog: Used for anonymous analytics tracking.

7. Data Security

  • All user data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not have access to your personal data or API keys.
  • No platform data is transmitted to our servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of updates.

9. Contact

If you have questions, please contact us via the support link in the extension.

By using Aria, you agree to this privacy policy.
