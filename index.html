Privacy Policy for LevelUp: AI Tools for LinkedIn

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

1. Introduction

LevelUp: AI Tools for LinkedIn (“the Extension”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a browser extension designed to help users create, improve, and manage LinkedIn content and messages using AI-powered tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your information is handled.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data or LinkedIn data on our servers. All data processed by the extension remains on your device.

  • OpenAI API Key: Your API key is stored securely in your browser’s local storage (or Chrome extension storage) and is never sent to any external server other than OpenAI’s API, as required for generating content.
  • Message History & Preferences: Any message history, settings, or preferences are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to us or any third party.
  • LinkedIn Data: The extension does not access, collect, or transmit your LinkedIn data or any other personal information.

3. Use of Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary for its features:

  • sidePanel: To display the extension’s interface in a side panel.
  • activeTab: To interact with the currently active tab for context menu actions.
  • storage: To save your settings, API key, and message history locally.
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click menu options for generating replies.

No unnecessary permissions are requested. No host permissions are requested unless required for future features.

4. Third-Party Services

OpenAI: When you use the AI features, your content and API key are sent directly from your device to OpenAI’s API for processing. Please review OpenAI’s Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Data Security

  • Your API key and data are stored only in your browser and are never shared with us or any third party (except OpenAI, as described above).
  • We do not have access to your data at any time.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the extension or on the extension’s listing page. Your continued use of the extension after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the extension’s data practices, please contact us via the support form linked in the extension’s settings.

By using LevelUp: AI Tools for LinkedIn, you agree to this privacy policy.
