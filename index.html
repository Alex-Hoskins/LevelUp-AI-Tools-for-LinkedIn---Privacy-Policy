Privacy Policy for LevelUp: AI Tools for LinkedIn

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

LevelUp: AI Tools for LinkedIn ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") is a browser extension designed to help users create, improve, and manage LinkedIn content and messages using AI-powered tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your information is handled.

2. Data Collection

We collect limited, anonymous usage data to improve the extension. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), message content, resume data, or sensitive LinkedIn data.

  • Anonymous Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics events (such as extension opened or feature used) to understand product usage and improve functionality. This data cannot be used to identify you.
  • OpenAI API Key: Your API key is stored only in your browser using Chrome local storage. It is never transmitted to LevelUp or any third-party server.
  • Local Storage Data: Your message history, resume text, templates, preferences, and profile-related data are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • LinkedIn Data (Read-Only, On-Demand):
    • Name and headline from profile pages are accessed only when needed to auto-fill templates and are not stored.
    • Job description text is accessed only when you click "Send to LevelUp" and is stored temporarily in your browser until the Resume Analyzer loads it.
    • The last received message is accessed only when you click "Generate Reply" and is stored temporarily in your browser until the AI Reply feature loads it.

3. How AI Features Work

  • All AI requests are sent directly from your browser to OpenAI using your API key.
  • LevelUp does not act as a proxy and does not see, store, or log your prompts or AI responses.

4. Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the extension.

  • Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • No message content, LinkedIn data, resume data, or API keys are included in analytics tracking.
  • No advertising networks are used.
  • Your data is never sold.

5. Use of Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary for its features:

  • scripting: Required to read LinkedIn page content for user-triggered features.
  • tabs: Required to detect when you're on relevant LinkedIn pages.
  • storage: To save your data locally in your browser.
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click actions for generating content.
  • sidePanel: To display the extension interface.
  • activeTab: To interact with the current page when you trigger a feature.

6. Third-Party Services

OpenAI: AI features send requests directly to OpenAI from your browser. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

PostHog: Used for anonymous analytics tracking as described above.

7. Data Security

  • All user data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not have access to your personal data, message content, or API keys.
  • Anonymous analytics data may be collected as described above.
  • No LinkedIn data is transmitted to our servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the extension or on the extension's listing page. Your continued use of the extension after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or data practices, please contact us via the support form linked in the extension.

By using LevelUp: AI Tools for LinkedIn, you agree to this privacy policy.
